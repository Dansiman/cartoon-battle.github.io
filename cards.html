Did you know you can embed those cards on your site? (click to see how!)

Usage

You can embed those cards on your site using an iframe:

<iframe src="{{ site.url }}/embed?Leela"/>

Just use /embed? address and the card name and optional level:

<iframe src="{{ site.url }}/embed?Leela 5*"/>

You can also make combos by combining two cards using + sign:

<iframe src="{{ site.url }}/embed?Leela 5* + Rifle 5**"/>
